Rules & Guidelines

To ensure effective healing and best results through naturopathy and yoga, all patients are requested to strictly follow the guidelines below.

1

Commitment to the Program

  • Follow the prescribed diet plan strictly without deviation
  • Practice daily yoga, pranayama and suggested natural therapies
  • Consistency is key for achieving results
2

Pre-Consultation Requirements

  • Submit all relevant medical reports before starting the program
  • Share complete medical history, medications, and lifestyle details
  • Inform about any allergies or existing conditions
3

Daily Monitoring

  • Send pictures of meals before eating (as instructed)
  • Provide daily updates on food intake, symptoms/changes and energy levels
  • Report any unusual reactions or discomfort immediately
4

Diet & Lifestyle Discipline

  • Avoid outside food, sugar, processed, and fried items
  • Follow meal timings strictly
  • Drink adequate water as prescribed
  • No alcohol, smoking, or harmful habits during the program
5

Yoga & Exercise Compliance

  • Perform all assigned yoga asanas and exercises regularly
  • Do not skip sessions without informing the doctor
  • Follow breathing exercises and meditation as guided
  • Avoid over-exertion or self-modified exercises
6

Sleep & Routine

  • Maintain proper sleep (6–8 hours minimum)
  • Follow a regular daily routine
  • Avoid late nights and irregular habits
7

Medication & Safety

  • Continue regular medications unless advised otherwise
  • Do not self-modify or stop medications
  • Inform immediately if any discomfort or unusual symptoms arise
8

Follow-Up & Communication

  • Attend all scheduled follow-up sessions on time
  • Respond promptly to doctor's messages and queries
  • Share progress reports and test results as requested
9

Consent & Responsibility

  • Results depend on individual commitment and body response
  • Naturopathy is a supportive therapy — do not discontinue prescribed medicines without doctor's advice
  • The doctor reserves the right to modify or discontinue the plan if guidelines are not followed
  • By booking, you agree to follow all program guidelines sincerely
📌 Note: Your cooperation and dedication are essential for achieving the best healing outcomes through natural methods.
